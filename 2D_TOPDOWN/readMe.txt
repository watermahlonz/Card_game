Controls 
Arrow keys to move
Space to shoot

Orange tiles represent the walls
Red cirlce are the enemies
Black tiles represent where the enemies will spawn
Blue square  gain gold or bonus points
Green square gain health
cyan square represents switch to open doors
Magenta square goal where the player wins the game

If health reaches zero the game will restart.
Enemies will follow the player but if colliding with a wall the enemy will get stuck.
